## The Gravitational Pillars
Astra is designed as a **"Stellar Map"** of work, where everything orbits around four foundational gravitational pillars:
1. **Context & Native Communication**: A unified semantic layer connecting documents, tasks, and built-in Slack-like departmental channels natively inside the platform.
2. **Execution**: Low-latency, fast workflows tailored for deep work across all departments.

## The Problem Surface
The modern knowledge worker, regardless of their department, operates in a highly fragmented tool ecosystem (using 9 to 10 apps daily, disjointed between chat, wikis, and task trackers). This excessive context-switching consumes up to 40% of productive time, inducing a severe cognitive tax.

## The Astra Solution
Astra physically solves this sociotechnical pathology. It acts algorithmically as a **Context Preserver** by bringing *all* communication (chat), storage (brainstorming/flowcharts), and execution (tasks) into a single, beautifully organized hub. By centralizing strategic macro-planning with micro-level execution and instant AI retrieval, Astra defends the organization's most valuable asset: its undivided attention.
